About Me

I'm an adaptable collaborator with a global mindset, having worked in the United States, Germany, and the Czech Republic. Passionate about life-long learning and solving complex problems in the data and software space. Early in my career I focused on turning data into actional information for stakeholders. My responsibilities included building data warehouses (and data marts) from the ground up, building and maintaining business-critical reporting, and consulting stakeholders in ambiguous environments to better understand data trends affecting their departments. Areas of focus were analyzing marketing impact, logistics efficiency, and supporting wholesale planning. I am now pivoting my career to software engineering while leveraging my experience in data. Graduating in spring 2024, I want to focus on developing software that helps push companies products forward. I particularly want to work in an environment where I can learn from experienced engineers to develop and solidify my own software engineering knowledge. I am interested in software design, helping build a large software project from the ground up, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
